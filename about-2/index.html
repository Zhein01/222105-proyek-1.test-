    About Hotel

    The Hotel Albatross is the right choice for visitors who are searching for a combination of charm, peace, quiet and a convenient position from where to explore surroundings.

    Splendid terrace, where you can relax and immerse yourself into upcoming morning of a new wonderful day in the atmosphere of Venetian daily life,

    On the ground floor, apart from the reception, there is a comfortable lounge where you can sit and drink tea or just read.

    It is a small comfortable hotel. Our staff offers an attentive high-quality service and is always ready to offer any help to guests. There is an amazing fusion of calm and tranquility at hotel with hectic outside.

    History of the Albatross
    Albatross has a rich history with many interesting facts and occasions that had an impact on its establishment. But through the whole period of its formation Spice was always liked by visitors.

    Albatross hitory begins in 1809 when a rich man named John Douglas bought a huge piece of land to fulfilled his father’s will – to build the place where people can relax and forget about their problems.
